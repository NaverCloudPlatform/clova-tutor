## Chatting with the AI Tutor

You can freely converse with the AI tutor to continue your learning.

---

## Setting Learning Goals

![Setting and Achieving Learning Goals](@site/docs/user-guide/english-tutor/img/학습목표설정과달성.gif)

- Click the 🎯 icon to set a target number of problems to solve (between 1 and 10).
- When you correctly solve problems recommended by the tutor, your goal progress increases.
- Once your progress reaches 100%, a celebration animation appears along with positive feedback from the tutor.
