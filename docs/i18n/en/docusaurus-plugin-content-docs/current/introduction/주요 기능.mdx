# Key Features

CLOVA Tutor provides AI tutors for **math** and **English**, allowing learners to experience **concept explanations, problem recommendations, step-by-step guidance, and reading practice** within a single chat interface.

Rather than relying on static content, CLOVA Tutor is built around **real-time, interactive dialogue**. Learning naturally flows through a continuous cycle of **questioning → problem solving → follow-up questions → review**, all within the same conversational context.

---

## Math Tutor

The Math Tutor supports learners through concept explanations, personalized problem recommendations, and guided problem-solving. By adapting explanations and difficulty based on learner responses, it encourages **self-directed learning** rather than passive answer consumption.

### Concept explanation and problem recommendation

Learners can ask about unfamiliar concepts and receive clear explanations in real time. Based on the conversation, the tutor then recommends **problems suited to the learner’s current level**, helping them immediately apply what they have learned.

Instead of stopping at an explanation, the interaction naturally continues through **recommended problems → related questions**, allowing learning to progress step by step.

### Guided, step-by-step problem solving

When a learner submits a problem, the tutor does not reveal the final answer upfront. Instead, it provides **progressive hints and explanations**, guiding the learner toward the solution while encouraging independent thinking.

Learners can ask questions at any point, and the tutor adjusts its guidance until the underlying concept is fully understood.

## English Tutor

The English Tutor supports vocabulary learning, grammar explanations, sentence-level reading, and practice problems. By adapting explanations to learner responses, it reduces the burden of studying alone and improves comprehension through **natural language dialogue**.

### Vocabulary explanation and reading practice

Learners can ask about unknown words or sentences to receive explanations of meaning and usage, then practice **sentence-by-sentence reading**. Follow-up questions can be asked immediately, allowing learners to clarify confusion as it arises.

## Learning Management

CLOVA Tutor supports **learning continuity** through goal setting and saved learning notes, ensuring that problem solving and review remain part of a connected experience.

- **Learning goals**: Learners can set goals within a chat, and the tutor provides goal-aware **feedback and guidance** throughout the conversation.
- **Learning notes**: Solved problems and explanations can be saved for later review. Saved problems can be **re-solved**, and learners can return to the **original chat context** where the problem was discussed.
- **Connected learning flow**: Chat history and learning notes remain linked, allowing review and re-solving to happen naturally without losing context. CLOVA Tutor is designed as a **supportive learning partner**, not a replacement for parents or teachers, helping reduce the burden of studying alone.
